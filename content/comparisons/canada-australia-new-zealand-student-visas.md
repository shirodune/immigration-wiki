# Canada, Australia, and New Zealand student visas

> Research comparison only; not legal advice. Rules depend on jurisdiction, application date, course, provider, personal circumstances, and individual visa or permit conditions.

## Scope and currency

- **Compared pathways:** Canadian study permit, Australian Student visa subclass 500, and New Zealand Fee Paying Student Visa
- **Last source check:** 2026-07-15
- **Currency warning:** Source effective dates are incomplete; operational amounts and conditions must be rechecked before use
- **Evidence quality:** Official government guidance in all three jurisdictions, plus an in-force Australian financial-capacity instrument; key Australian Home Affairs pages were access-restricted

## Financial evidence details

Canada requires proof for tuition, living costs, transportation, and accompanying family without relying on work in Canada. The reviewed page requires first-year resources and a plan for the full duration of longer programs, but this wiki did not preserve the annually updated living threshold. ^[raw/articles/canada-ircc-study-permit-financial-support.md]

Australia’s reviewed, in-force instrument provides the strongest dated legal source in this comparison. It specifies AUD$29,710 in annual living costs for a covered primary applicant, with separate partner, child, school, income-route, and pro-rata rules. ^[raw/articles/australia-financial-capacity-instrument-subclass-500.md]

New Zealand’s live guidance displayed NZD$20,000 per year or NZD$1,667 per month for tertiary and other non-compulsory study, with different school-student amounts and separate outward-travel requirements. The page did not state an effective date. ^[raw/articles/new-zealand-student-fund-requirements.md]

## Interpretation

- The headline work limits are not directly interchangeable: Canada and New Zealand express weekly limits, Australia uses a fortnightly limit, and all depend on eligibility and individual conditions.
- None of the three systems allows prospective students to assume that future employment replaces pre-application financial evidence.
- Nominal fund amounts should not be ranked without considering currency, tuition, family composition, duration, included costs, evidence rules, and effective dates.
- Australia has the strongest primary legal source in this set for financial capacity, but its operational evidence is less complete because Home Affairs blocked direct automated review.
- New Zealand’s compared pathway is specifically the Fee Paying Student Visa, while the Canadian and Australian pages describe broader national student categories.
