# New Zealand — Immigration New Zealand Student fund requirements

## Citation

Immigration New Zealand. “Student fund requirements.” Accessed 2026-07-15. [Original page](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/process-to-apply/applying-for-a-visa/providing-evidence-and-documents-to-support-your-visa-application/student-fund-requirements/).

## Authority and scope

Immigration New Zealand is the government visa authority. This page explains student-visa living-cost, outward-travel, funding-source, and evidence requirements.

## Original summary

The page says tertiary, English-language, and other non-compulsory students must show NZD$20,000 for each year or NZD$1,667 for each month when study is shorter than a year. For school years 1–13, it shows NZD$17,000 per year or NZD$1,417 per month. It separately requires proof of outward travel or money to buy a ticket, and additional maternity-care funds where applicable. Funds must come from a verifiable source and be genuinely available, potentially through the applicant, an acceptable sponsor, or a guarantor or third party.
