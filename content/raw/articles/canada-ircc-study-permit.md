# Canada — IRCC Study permit

## Citation

Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada. “Study permit.” Government of Canada. Issued 2024-01-19 and modified 2026-04-24 in page metadata. Accessed 2026-07-15. [Original page](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit.html).

## Authority and scope

IRCC is the Canadian federal department responsible for immigration and citizenship administration. This official landing page introduces the Canadian study-permit process and links to eligibility, document, application, arrival, and study-period guidance.

## Original summary

The page describes a study permit as an IRCC-issued document that allows foreign nationals to study at designated learning institutions in Canada. It says most foreign nationals need one, advises applicants to obtain the required documents and apply before travelling, and directs readers to detailed process sections. On the access date, it displayed a CAN$150 study-permit fee and stated that processing times vary by country.
