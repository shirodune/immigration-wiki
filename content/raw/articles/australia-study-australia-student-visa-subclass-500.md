# Australia — Study Australia Student visa subclass 500

## Citation

Study Australia, Australian Government. “Student visa (subclass 500).” Accessed 2026-07-15. [Original page](https://www.studyaustralia.gov.au/en/plan-your-move/your-guide-to-visas/student-visa-subclass-500).

## Authority and scope

Study Australia is an Australian Government international-education service. This page gives a directly accessible operational overview of the Student visa (subclass 500), while referring readers to Home Affairs and VEVO for authoritative application and individual-condition details.

## Original summary

The page says the visa covers multiple education sectors and requires enrolment with an Australian provider and a Confirmation of Enrolment before applying. It describes study, family inclusion, travel, a maximum five-year stay, work rights, fees, and key conditions. On the access date, it stated that most student-visa holders may work up to 48 hours per fortnight while study is in session, while students who have started a master’s degree by research or doctoral degree have no work-hour limit. It displayed application fees from AUD$2,500 from 1 July 2026.
