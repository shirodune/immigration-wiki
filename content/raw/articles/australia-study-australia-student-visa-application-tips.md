# Australia — Study Australia student-visa application tips

## Citation

Study Australia, Australian Government. “6 tips for a better Student visa application for Australia.” 2025-05-21. Accessed 2026-07-15. [Original page](https://www.studyaustralia.gov.au/en/tools-and-resources/tips-and-advice-for-students/6-tips-for-a-better-student-visa-application-for-australia).

## Authority and scope

Study Australia is an Australian Government international-education service. This advisory page summarizes application preparation and points applicants to Home Affairs tools and requirements.

## Original summary

The page identifies a valid Confirmation of Enrolment, sufficient-funds evidence, evidence addressing the Genuine Student requirement, English-language test results where applicable, and Overseas Student Health Cover as common subclass 500 requirements. It recommends using the Home Affairs Document Checklist Tool, providing complete and translated documents, and supporting Genuine Student answers with specific evidence rather than generic statements.

## Consequential claims supported

- **Claim:** A subclass 500 application may require a CoE, financial proof, Genuine Student evidence, English results where applicable, and OSHC.
  - **Jurisdiction:** Australia
  - **Applicable date:** Article dated 2025-05-21; current status not separately stated
  - **Accessed:** 2026-07-15
  - **Limitations:** The applicant-specific Home Affairs checklist and current law determine exactly which evidence is required.
- **Claim:** The Home Affairs Document Checklist Tool is tailored to passport country and education provider.
  - **Jurisdiction:** Australia
  - **Applicable date:** Article dated 2025-05-21
  - **Accessed:** 2026-07-15
  - **Limitations:** The tool and Home Affairs pages were access-restricted during this research; users must check the live tool directly.

## Limitations

This is practical guidance, not the controlling visa rule or a complete checklist. It predates the access date by more than a year and should be checked against current Home Affairs requirements. The linked Genuine Student and document-checklist pages were blocked from automated review.
