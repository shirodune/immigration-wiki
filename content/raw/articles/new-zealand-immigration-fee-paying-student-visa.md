# New Zealand — Immigration New Zealand Fee Paying Student Visa

## Citation

Immigration New Zealand. “Fee Paying Student Visa.” Accessed 2026-07-15. [Original page](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/visas/fee-paying-student-visa/).

## Authority and scope

Immigration New Zealand is the government service responsible for New Zealand visa guidance and processing. This page covers the Fee Paying Student Visa rather than every New Zealand student-visa category.

## Original summary

The page describes a visa for international students paying tuition themselves or with family, loan, or partial-scholarship support. It requires an offer of place from an approved education provider and describes tuition and living-fund evidence, insurance, health and character requirements, online application, visa duration, travel, and conditional work rights. It also explains that partners and dependent children cannot be included in the same application but may apply for relationship-based visas.

## Consequential claims supported

- **Claim:** Applicants must have an offer of place from an approved education provider and show sufficient tuition and living-cost support or qualifying alternatives.
  - **Jurisdiction:** New Zealand
  - **Applicable date:** Not stated on page
  - **Accessed:** 2026-07-15
  - **Limitations:** Evidence requirements and acceptable sponsorship or scholarship arrangements need verification against the detailed document sections and the applicant’s circumstances.
- **Claim:** The visa can permit part-time work for up to 25 hours a week while studying and full-time work in holidays, depending on the visa conditions.
  - **Jurisdiction:** New Zealand
  - **Applicable date:** Not stated on page
  - **Accessed:** 2026-07-15
  - **Limitations:** This is conditional, not a universal entitlement; the individual visa conditions control.
- **Claim:** The page displayed a maximum stay of four years, a cost from NZD$850, and an 80% processing estimate of eight weeks.
  - **Jurisdiction:** New Zealand
  - **Applicable date:** Observed 2026-07-15; no separate effective date stated
  - **Accessed:** 2026-07-15
  - **Limitations:** Visa duration depends on paid study and passport validity. Fees and processing estimates are dynamic operational figures and must be rechecked.

## Limitations

The page is operational guidance for one visa category, not a complete statement of New Zealand immigration law or every student pathway. Individual conditions, course and provider approval, age, family circumstances, health, character, and evidence can change the result. The page did not expose a clear modification or effective date during review, so access-date currency must not be treated as a legal commencement date.
